Are you feeling burnt out and exhausted from all the demands and stresses in your life? Do you struggle to understand or name your feelings, needs, and desires? Maybe you’re challenged in your relationships to communicate freely, and you often find yourself feeling disconnected, unheard or unsafe. It’s hard to feel good when you’re feeling depleted and overwhelmed. Stress, trauma and loss can make it hard to stay on top of our responsibilities and manage problems like anxiety and depression. Despite the challenges that have gotten you down lately, you don’t have to be stuck here. You can heal all parts of you and reclaim your sense of wholeness and worthiness. You’re not alone in feeling this way!

Meet Erva Kambo, LP

I am a licensed psychologist Florence-based (Italy). I like to help people feel understood, face their pain and joy, and find inner strength in difficult moments of their lives. Through a relationship based on trust and empathy it will be possible to create a safe and welcoming space in which you will have the opportunity to deeply explore yourself and develop greater awareness of your emotions, thoughts and behaviors. Together, we’ll explore issues related to family, work, identity, gender, race and culture. I can help you move towards a version of your life where emotional and physical discomfort no longer dictate your decisions. Join me in a process of discovery and growth!

I currently provide services in italian, english and albanian!
